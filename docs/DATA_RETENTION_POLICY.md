# Data Retention Policy

This Data Retention Policy explains how GIA manages temporary storage, retention, and deletion of processed information and generated reports.

---

## Purpose

GIA follows a minimal-retention approach intended to:

- Support analytics processing
- Enable report generation
- Maintain platform stability and security
- Reduce unnecessary long-term storage of submitted data

---

## Temporary Processing Data

Submitted TikTok links, usernames, analytics information, and generated reports may be temporarily processed and cached to:

- Generate dashboards
- Produce exports
- Improve processing performance

Temporary processing data may be automatically deleted after processing completion or after a limited retention period.

---

## Exported Reports

Generated exports, including PDF and PNG reports:

- May be temporarily stored to allow downloading
- May be automatically removed after export completion or expiration periods

Users are responsible for storing downloaded copies externally.

---

## Technical Logs & Security Data

Limited operational and security-related logs may be retained temporarily for:

- Platform diagnostics
- Abuse prevention
- Security monitoring
- Performance optimization

---

## Data Minimization

GIA aims to minimize unnecessary collection and retention of:

- Personal information
- User-identifiable data
- Long-term analytics storage

The platform currently does not require persistent user accounts.

---

## Security Measures

Retained data is protected using reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to prevent:

- Unauthorized access
- Data misuse
- Accidental disclosure

---

## Policy Updates

This Data Retention Policy may be updated periodically as platform operations and infrastructure evolve.
